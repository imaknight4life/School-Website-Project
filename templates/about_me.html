About Martin Andrews

Where craftsmanship meets light — a lifetime of transforming molten glass into meaning.

Meet the Artist

Martin Andrews is an accomplished glass artist and the founder of Andrews Glassworks, established in 1991. A graduate of West Surrey College, he blends fine art and design expertise to craft unique glass creations that merge technical mastery with emotional storytelling.

Bespoke Glass for Every Space

Martin’s work brings colour and character to a wide range of settings:

  • Schools: Decorative etched windows.
  • Hospitals: Commemorative coloured windows.
  • Private & Commercial: Bespoke designs for homes, hotels, and businesses.
  • Churches: Intricate architectural glasswork with spiritual depth.

Featured Work: A 2.5-meter architectural cross for St. John’s Church — six laminated glass layers, engraved with silver “tears” and hands based on the congregation.

Innovation in Glass

Through Andrews Glassworks, Martin continues to push the boundaries of the craft — shaping molten glass into vibrant forms that celebrate the harmony of colour, form, and light.

Our Journey Through the Years

From humble beginnings to timeless craftsmanship — explore our story below.

1998 — The Beginning

Martin Andrews Glass began as a small workshop focused on creating bespoke glass pieces by hand.

2005 — Expansion

We expanded our workshop and introduced new cutting and shaping technologies, blending tradition with innovation.

2015 — Showroom Opens

Our modern showroom opened to the public, showcasing our growing range of custom glasswork and designs.

Today — Continuing the Legacy

Now, Martin Andrews Glass stands as a trusted name across the UK for quality craftsmanship and creative design.
