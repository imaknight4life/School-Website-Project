Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior experience?

No experience is required. Beginners are very welcome.

Can I take home what I make?

Yes — all pieces can be collected after cooling (usually next day).

Is there an age requirement?

The minimum age is 12, and under-18s must be accompanied by an adult.

Do you allow gift vouchers?

Yes! Gift vouchers are available for all lesson types.
